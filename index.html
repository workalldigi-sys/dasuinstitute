About DASU Institute

DASU Institute of Commerce & Competitive Exams has been a beacon of quality education in Visakhapatnam since 1992. With over three decades of academic excellence, we have established ourselves as the premier coaching institute for competitive examinations and commerce education in the region.

Our institute specializes in providing comprehensive coaching for various government job examinations including Defence, SSC, Banking, Railways, and State Government services, alongside quality education in Commerce and Management subjects. We pride ourselves on our disciplined approach, concept clarity, and strategic exam preparation that has helped thousands of students achieve their career goals.
